01 / About

Who I Am

I'm a final-year B.Tech Electronics & Telecommunication student with Honors in VLSI at VPKBIET. My focus lies at the intersection of hardware design and AI — designing fast, efficient silicon that powers the next generation of intelligent systems.

I've designed and implemented a 32-bit RISC-V processor on FPGA, built high-performance pipelined accelerators, and I'm currently working on an AI-enabled RISC-V SoC for autonomous drone systems.

When I'm not in simulation, I'm reading papers on computer architecture, contributing to open-source hardware, or mentoring juniors in digital design.
